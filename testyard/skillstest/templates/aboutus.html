About TestYard

Welcome to TestYard©, where we are passionate about empowering individuals and organizations through skills testing. With 20 years of combined experience in the software development industry, our team of experts is dedicated to creating a seamless platform that enables you to assess and enhance your skills effectively.

At TestYard, we understand the challenges that both job seekers and employers face in today's competitive market. Finding the right talent or proving your capabilities can be a daunting task. That's why we have developed a comprehensive and user-friendly skills testing solution that simplifies the process and delivers accurate results.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between candidates and employers by providing a robust platform that enables fair and objective evaluations. We believe that skills testing should be accessible to all, regardless of location, educational background, or previous work experience. Through our website and mobile application, we aim to democratize skill assessment and create equal opportunities for everyone.

We also offer our platform to educational institutions for their requirements in skills testing. We understand the varied requirements of various fields of study (like engineering and technical disciplines, arts and humanities, etc) and we are constantly in the process of improving our platform in order to serve all disciplines. For example, we are currently working on making this platform available to engineering disciplines for their Engineering Drawing tests (which has very specific and singular requirements).

What sets us apart is our commitment to delivering a high-quality service that is tailored to meet the diverse needs of various industries and disciplines. In addition to providing this platform as a service (PaaS), we also allow educators and assessors with the choice of monetizing their library of tests and interviews by using the skills networking facility that our service provides. Using it, one may create paid groups of users who may be offered their premium tests and interviews.

We also understand the importance of data privacy and security. Rest assured that your personal information and test results are treated with the utmost confidentiality. We adhere to stringent data protection measures, and our platform is designed to safeguard your privacy at every step.

Whether you are a job seeker looking to showcase your skills, a student looking to assess your skills, or an employer or a educator assessing talent, TestYard is your trusted partner. Join thousands of individuals and organizations who have already benefited from our platform and taken their careers or recruitment processes to the next level. Thank you for choosing TestYard. We are excited to embark on this journey with you and support your goals of success and growth. And of course, should you come across any issues, do please scream at support@testyard.com. We promise to lend you an ear and help you with your issue too.


Good luck!

The TestYard Team.
